# Research Report: Sean Strickland's Last UFC Fight (UFC 312)

## Key Questions & Findings  

### **1. What are the key facts about Sean Strickland's last UFC fight?**  

**Direct Answer:**  
Sean Strickland lost a unanimous decision (50-45, 50-45, 49-46) to Dricus Du Plessis in their middleweight title rematch at **UFC 312** (February 8, 2025). The fight was one-sided, with Du Plessis breaking Strickland’s nose and outstriking him (149 total strikes). Strickland struggled defensively and failed to mount significant offense.  

**Supporting Evidence:**  
- **Outcome:** Unanimous decision loss ([Source 3](https://www.ufc.com/event/ufc-312), [Source 5](https://www.aljazeera.com/sports/2025/2/9/dricus-du-plessis-beats-sean-strickland-defends-title-at-ufc-312)).  
- **Key Moment:** Du Plessis broke Strickland’s nose in the fourth round ([Source 2](https://www.espn.com/mma/fighter/_/id/3093653/sean-strickland), [Source 8](https://sports.yahoo.com/ufc-312-results-dricus-du-plessis-badly-breaks-sean-stricklands-nose-in-lopsided-title-rematch-063807880.html)).  
- **Stats:** Du Plessis landed 149 strikes; Strickland offered little counter offense ([Source 8](https://sports.yahoo.com/ufc-312-results-dricus-du-plessis-badly-breaks-sean-stricklands-nose-in-lopsided-title-rematch-063807880.html)).  

**Conflicting Information:**

**Supporting Evidence:**
